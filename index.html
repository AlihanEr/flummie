About Flummie

Welcome to Flummie, where passion for art meets the timeless beauty of candles. Each flower candle is meticulously handcrafted to bring warmth, elegance, and a touch of nature into your home.

Every creation is unique, made with premium wax and carefully sculpted to resemble the delicate petals of real flowers. Whether for decoration, gifts, or special occasions, Flummie candles are designed to captivate and inspire.

Thank you for supporting handmade artistry and bringing a piece of floral beauty into your life.
